About Uncork & Fork

At Uncork & Fork, the spotlight is on the wine. Every bottle tells a story, and the food you pair it with can either enhance or dull its voice. That’s why we flip the traditional approach and let the wine choose dinner.

It’s pairing made simple, delicious, and always centered around the star of the table: your wine.

How It Works

- Choose a wine from the dropdown menu.

- The app analyzes your wine and fetches dish pairings using curated categories and the Spoonacular wine API.

- For each paired dish, the app retrieves recipe suggestions with ingredients and step-by-step instructions...

...And all that’s left is to cook the dish, pour your wine, and enjoy a perfectly paired meal.
